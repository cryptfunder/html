Where is Cryptfunder incorporated?

Cryptfunder is established in the Cayman Islands jurisdiction. The Cayman Islands are a popular international jurisdiction for the incorporation of blockchain companies and related fund activities.

The Cayman Islandsjurisdiction has seen a strong increase in private equity funds year on year over the past decade. The popularity of Cayman private equity funds has been fuelled by the increase in hedge fund managers growing into the private equity space and by the increased use of private equity funds to pursue distressed asset investments.

The Cayman Islands are ideally suited for use as a closed-end investment vehicle. In particular, one which seeks investment during a capital raising period (in our case, the ICO) and draws down periodically in order to fund investment opportunities and operating costs when they arise. This was established under legislation that was drafted with the key terms of private equity vehicles in mind.

Why is Cryptfunder on the blockchain?

There are many reasons to use blockchain technology for Cryptfunder, among those are:

  • Full transparency over ICO funding
  • Transparency over ICO funding tokens received, held and/or sold
  • Escrow activities and exchange of crypto funds
  • Easy exchange of tokens
  • Holding of tokens for CF30 token portfolio of 30 tokens only holds cryptocurrency assets

How do I participate in the ICO?

To join our ICO:

  • Step 1: Open a wallet through Metamask, MyEtherWallet or another compatible wallet. Safely store the information for your private key and address.
  • Step 2: Buy ETH through a recognized exchange and send it to your wallet.
  • Step 3: Click on the "Join ICO" button at the top of our cryptfunder.io website.
  • Step 4: Fill in the register form with your information and your public Ethereum wallet address or login to your account.
  • Step 5: Once you are in the Customer Dashboard, you’ll be able to see your account info, wallet info and balances.
  • Step 6: Once your deposits populate, you’ll be able to purchase CFND once the crowdsale stages go live. Please allow adequate time for your crypto to transact and verify before contacting us concerning your deposits.
